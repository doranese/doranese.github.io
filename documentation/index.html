Doranese Documentation

Doranese is a language intended to use as few latin base characters in a different writing syllabary. The following characters are the eleven characters of the language. Accented sounds do not apply to the final count.

  • A a
    • Ą ą -- Long low tone
    • Ā ā -- Long medium tone
    • Ă ă -- Long high tone
  • E e
    • Ę ę -- Long low tone
    • Ē ē -- Long medium tone
    • &Ebreve; &ebreve; -- Long high tone
  • i
    • Į į -- Long low tone
    • Ī ī -- Long medium tone
    • &Ibreve; &ibreve; -- Long high tone
  • o
    • &Oogon; &oogon; -- Long low tone
    • Ō ō -- Long medium tone
    • &Obreve; &obreve; -- Long high tone
  • u
    • Ų ų -- Long low tone
    • Ū ū -- Long medium tone
    • Ŭ ŭ -- Long high tone
  • b
  • d
  • j
  • s
  • n
  • r
